Tax Incentives

Residential Energy Property Credit: 25D Tax Credit

Expiration Date: The 30% credit for clean energy systems like solar panels, wind turbines, geothermal systems, and battery storage will now expire after December 31, 2025. This is a significant acceleration of the credit's termination compared to the IRA, which had scheduled a gradual phase-out througsh 2034.

No Phase-Out: Unlike the gradual reduction in credit percentage envisioned by the IRA, the OBBBA abruptly ends the 25D credit at the end of 2025, meaning homeowners installing systems in 2026 and beyond won't receive any credit.

System Installation Deadline: To qualify for the 30% credit, homeowners must have their eligible systems installed and operational by December 31, 2025.

Overview

Earn up to 30% of Costs in Tax Credits for Qualified Energy Installations

Homeowners who invest in renewable energy for their home, such as geothermal heat pumps, solar electric panels and water heaters, wind turbines, fuel cells, or battery storage technology, are eligible for a tax credit.

Status

Available now

Qualified improvements

Clean energy property must meet the following standards to qualify for the residential clean energy credit:

  • Geothermal heat pumps: Meet ENERGY STAR® requirements in effect at time of purchase
  • Solar water heaters: Certified by the Solar Rating & Certification Corporation or a comparable entity endorsed by your state
  • Battery storage technology: Have a capacity of at least three kilowatt hours
